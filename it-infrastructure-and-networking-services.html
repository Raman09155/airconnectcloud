What is IT Infrastructure as a Service?

IT Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) is an example of cloud computing that provides virtualized computing resources via a cloud service provider over the internet. The IaaS model is a boon for businesses as it spares them the significant upfront capital investments in on-premise hardware as it is provided over the cloud. Servers, cloud storage, networking and other resources are available as a service.

LaaS has greatly improved the ability of many organizations to scale, reduced their cost of operations, and granted them greater flexibility. IaaS servers and services can host applications, store data, and run workloads on hybrid environments. IaaS models and services help organizations to capture business agility and common market adaptive business flexibility.
