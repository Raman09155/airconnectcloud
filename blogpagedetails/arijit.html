Arijit Singh Live at Zomaland: A Magical Night of Music and Connectivity

ACIPL · Entertainment · April 25

The Zomaland experience reached new heights with Arijit Singh's soul-stirring live performance, enchanting thousands of fans under the stars. Held in a vibrant, festival-style setting, the event brought together music, food, art, and entertainment — and ACIPL was at the heart of it, powering the entire venue with world-class IT infrastructure and high-speed internet.

“An unforgettable musical journey deserves flawless technology. We ensured every beat, every moment, reached the audience without a hitch.”

Complete IT Infrastructure for a Seamless Experience

From high-capacity internet connectivity to reliable on-ground technical support, ACIPL enabled smooth operations across all event segments — ticketing counters, security teams, media zones, and artist areas. With structured cabling and secure networking, we ensured that Zomaland's digital ecosystem stayed strong and steady throughout the night.

Live Streaming, Real-Time Coordination & Crowd Engagement

Our high-speed connectivity empowered media crews to live stream the event, while backstage coordination and vendor management ran effortlessly via ACIPL’s robust network. Fans shared the magic in real-time, making the event a trending sensation across social platforms.

With every note that echoed through the crowd and every connection we enabled, ACIPL proved once again that when music meets technology, the experience becomes truly unforgettable.
