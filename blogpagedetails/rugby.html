Powering Passion: Rugby Championship Dehradun 2025

ACIPL · Sports · July 08

Set against the scenic backdrop of Uttarakhand, the Rugby Championship in Dehradun brought together energy, excitement, and athleticism. The event saw teams from across the region compete with unmatched determination, delivering high-octane matches that kept spectators on the edge of their seats.

“Rugby is more than a game — it’s a community of strength, spirit, and support. This championship truly brought that spirit to life in Dehradun.”

Reliable Connectivity, Seamless Coverage

ACIPL was proud to support the Rugby Championship by providing robust and uninterrupted internet services throughout the venue. From live streaming to on-site coordination, our network ensured everything ran smoothly behind the scenes—so the players and organizers could focus on the game.

A Day of Unity, Performance & Technology

Beyond the fierce competition, the event highlighted unity, team spirit, and modern sports infrastructure. With ACIPL’s high-speed internet in place, organizers enabled real-time match updates, efficient coordination, and a connected experience for attendees, press, and participants alike.
