Top 10 Data Center Companies in India (2025 Updated List)

ACIPL · Information · 25 Sep 2025

Connect Infosys ACIPL is a specialist in cloud and data center management. IT infrastructure solutions that allow companies to create resilient and adaptable digital environments. In the blog below, you will learn about the most influential players that shape India's data center market by following our in-depth guide to the top 10 data center companies in India and the most trustworthy companies for data centers in India.

Introduction to Data Centers in India
  • India has been recognized as one of the most digitally driven economies around the globe, and with this rapid expansion comes a growing demand for high-quality data storage as well as high-speed connectivity. Data centers play a vital part in assisting enterprises as well as the cloud computing industry, providing services for IT as well as digital platforms. As tech giants across the world as well as Indian companies continue to grow their operations, the field of data centers is experiencing rapid growth thanks to technological advances in infrastructure, expansion of infrastructure, and government initiatives that promote digital transformation. This is the reason knowing the top 10 data center companies in India, as well as other listed data center companies in India, is crucial for companies trying to remain relevant in a digital world.
Why Data Centers Are Important for Businesses
  • Modern data centers form the core of today's digital-first society. They provide security and scalable infrastructure to manage huge quantities of data. They allow banks' and sites' banking operations, as well as communications, to operate smoothly all day long. The rapid growth of cloud computing and artificial intelligence (AI), along with the Internet of Things (IoT), has led to businesses becoming more dependent on modern data centers to provide effective data management and disaster recovery as well as backup options. The top 10 data center companies in India, as well as other listed data center management companies in India, offer essential services to ensure enhanced network performance, safe information storage, and adherence to regulatory requirements in the BFSI, healthcare, and telecom sectors.

Factors to Consider When Choosing a Data Center Provider

The selection of the best data center provider is essential in ensuring continuity of business and capacity. When looking at providers in the top 10 data center companies in India or selecting from the list of data center firms in India, companies should take into account:

  • Locality: Close proximity to hubs of business for lower latency and speedier data access
  • Security: Strong physical security measures as well as cybersecurity protocols
  • Guaranteed Uptime: A high level of Service Level Agreements (SLAs) guaranteeing the least amount of downtime
  • Scalability: A flexible infrastructure that can accommodate the ever-growing demands for data
  • Connectivity: Comprehensive network options, multi-carrier carrier service, and cloud connectivity
  • Cost Efficiency: Price competitive and aligned with the broadest range of service offerings

List of Top 10 Data Center Companies in India

  • STT Global Data Centers India Pvt Ltd
    It was established in 2017 and is located in Mumbai. STT Global Data Centers India Pvt Ltd is one of the top 10 data center companies in India and among the most trusted companies for data centers in India. It has several modern facilities in key cities that offer flexible cloud hosting and colocation, along with managed and unmanaged solutions. STT GDC's robust system of infrastructure as well as security structures helps telecom operators and enterprises with high reliability.
  • CtrlS Data Centers Ltd.
    In 2008, the company was founded with its headquarters in Hyderabad. CtrlS Data Centers Ltd is Asia's biggest Tier-4-certified data center service provider. It is featured among the top 10 data center companies in India and also among the top-rated companies that operate data centers in India. It is renowned for its 99.995 percent uptime and its ability to recover from disasters. CtrlS caters to BFSI, healthcare, government, and telecom industries that require critical support.
  • NTT Communications
    NTT Communications, with global roots dating to 1999 and an Indian headquarters located in Mumbai, is listed as one of the top 10 data center companies in India as well as a prominent name among the companies that are listed as data centers in India. The facilities it operates, located in Mumbai, Bengaluru, and Noida, provide enterprise-grade hybrid cloud, colocation, and IT solutions sought after by multinational corporations that require the most high-end connectivity to networks.
  • Tata Communications Ltd.
    An enviable company since 1986, Tata Communications Ltd is headquartered in Mumbai and is in the top 10 companies in the field of data centers in India and is an important member of the top companies that operate data centers in India. With a global subsea fiber system, Tata Communications offers secure cloud services and colocation along with data center options known for their efficiency and scaling.
  • Sify Technologies
    In 1995, Sify Technologies was founded and is headquartered within Chennai. Sify Technologies holds an excellent position in the top 10 data center companies in India and is regarded as one of the notable listed companies in the field of data centers in India. Sify Technologies' facilities are able to allow edge computing, hybrid cloud colocation, and edge computing, as its managed services aid companies achieve cost-efficiency and a seamless digital transformation.
  • Nxtra Data Limited
    Nxtra Data Limited, a Bharti Airtel subsidiary founded in 2015, is headquartered at Gurgaon (Gurugram) and is listed among the top 10 data center companies in India and is a major participant in the list of companies that operate data centers in India. It is a world-class, energy-efficient data center that is committed to sustainability. It also supports startups and enterprises with flexible cloud and hosting solutions.
  • Reliance Group
    It was founded in 1966 and is headquartered in Mumbai. Reliance Group ranks in the top 10 data center companies in India and is a prominent name in the community of companies that are listed as data centers in India. The company is known for its massive technological infrastructure investment. Reliance provides secure data hosting, high-capacity solutions, and services that are compliant with a broad spectrum of industries.
  • Web Werks India
    Established in 1996 in the city of Mumbai, Web Werks India is part of the top 10 data center companies in India and is a reputable part of the top businesses in the data center industry of India. The facilities are strategically located and offer cloud computing, colocation, and disaster recovery services for companies. Web Werks' partnerships with international technology companies enhance its offerings of services.
  • AdaniConnex
    AdaniConnex was founded in 2021 and has its headquarters in Ahmedabad. It is an alliance of Adani Enterprises and EdgeConneX. It is a fast-growing competitor among the top 10 data center companies in India. AdaniConnex also shines as one of the most promising companies operating in the data center industry in India. It focuses on renewable energy-powered, flexible data centers that can help India's growing digital economy.
  • ESDS Software Solution Limited
    Established in 2005 and located with its headquarters in Nashik, ESDS Software Solution Limited is a well-known company among the top 10 data center companies in India and is a prominent participant among the listed companies operating data centers in India. It offers cloud-based hosting and managed IT and security services. It is distinguished by its unique auto-scalable cloud platform that is designed for SMEs as well as large corporations.

Future of Data Centers in India

  • The digital transformation sweeping across India is accelerating the demand for data centers to record levels. With the advent of 5G technology, automated automation, AI-driven, and cloud-based apps are becoming the norm, and the top 10 data center companies in India, together with other data center firms in India, are advancing their infrastructure and capabilities. Policies of the government that promote localization of data sustainability and cyber resilience drive investment in secure and energy-efficient data centers. The constant interest in hybrid cloud solutions as well as edge computing suggests that the Indian data center market is likely to experience rapid growth and great technological advancement in the next few years.
