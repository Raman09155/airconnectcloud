Khelo India Bihar: Uniting Youth Sports with Next-Gen Connectivity

The Khelo India Youth Games in Bihar brought together budding athletes from across the nation in a celebration of energy, competition, and national pride. With thousands of participants and spectators, the event needed more than just sportsmanship—it needed seamless coordination backed by rock-solid connectivity.

“Sports empower youth. Technology connects them. Khelo India in Bihar was a shining example of both coming together with impact.”

ACIPL Powers the Digital Backbone of the Games

As the official IT infrastructure and internet service partner, ACIPL ensured uninterrupted connectivity across the venue. From high-speed internet for live broadcasting and press operations to stable internal networks for event management and coordination, our solutions elevated the experience for athletes, organizers, and audiences alike.

Seamless Execution through End-to-End IT Support

ACIPL deployed on-ground technical teams and advanced network infrastructure to guarantee zero downtime throughout the event. Our proactive monitoring, dedicated support, and high-capacity connectivity played a critical role in enabling digital communication, registration systems, real-time score updates, and secure media zones.

With events like Khelo India growing year by year, ACIPL continues to play a behind-the-scenes role in making them future-ready—ensuring that India's youth shine on the field, while we handle everything behind the scenes.
