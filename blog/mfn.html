MFN Noida: Combat Thrills Backed by Seamless Connectivity

ACIPL · Sports · August 02

Matrix Fight Night (MFN) returned to Noida with another high-voltage edition that blended raw power, precision, and passion. Fans poured into the venue to witness India’s top MMA talent go head-to-head in a night of unforgettable action and sportsmanship.

“More than just a fight night — MFN in Noida was a connected, coordinated, and electrifying experience, both inside and outside the cage.”

ACIPL Ensures a Technologically Flawless Event

Behind the punches and spotlights, ACIPL powered the event with robust internet services and complete IT infrastructure. From live streaming setups and media coverage to internal coordination, our network backbone ensured zero downtime and full digital reliability throughout the night.

Empowering India's MMA Stars with Technology

MFN continues to be a springboard for India’s emerging MMA talent, and this Noida chapter was no exception. With our on-ground IT team managing network stability and connectivity, organizers and production crews executed every detail with precision—offering a world-class experience for athletes, staff, and spectators alike.

Whether it's in the ring or behind the scenes, ACIPL remains committed to supporting India's biggest sports and entertainment platforms with next-gen digital infrastructure and flawless service delivery.
