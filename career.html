Network Engineer

Salary range : 20-30k

Job Location : New delhi (WFO)

Qualification : BE/BeTech/

Job Description :

  • Network Engineer with 4+ years of experience in designing, configuring, and troubleshooting network infrastructure.
  • Extensive knowledge of networking technologies like TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, VPN, and Firewall configurations.
  • Proficient in network hardware, including routers, switches, firewalls, and load balancers (Cisco, Juniper, etc.).
  • Hands-on experience with network protocols, network security, and performance optimization techniques.
  • Strong understanding of network monitoring and management tools (e.g., Wireshark, SolarWinds, Nagios).
  • Excellent knowledge of routing and switching protocols like OSPF, BGP, and EIGRP.
  • Experience in implementing and managing network virtualization technologies such as SD-WAN and NFV.
  • Proven ability to troubleshoot complex network issues and perform root cause analysis under high-pressure environments.
